Activities

The farm park has many activities for people to do. Some of these activities are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze.

You can also:

  • Visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat.
  • Play with farm tools, such as a pitchfork or shovel (safely!).
  • Pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!
